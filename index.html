Huloop is a new calendaring feature on Hulu that allows

you to schedule watch parties with your loved ones.

Huloop syncs with your calendar and creates a

personalized event to share with your family and friends.

*Available for subscribers on the following Hulu (no Ads)

or Hulu (no Ads) + Live TV*
